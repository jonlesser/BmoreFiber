Baltimore is home to world-renowned research laboratories and Federal facilities, and neighbor to our nation's capital. Baltimore offers Google Fiber a showcase to the world and access to a key market.

Baltimore's proximity to Washington, D.C. and its position within the northeast corridor provides tremendous visibility to the Google Fiber for Communities program and strategic opportunities for the Baltimore trial in particular.

Federal decision-makers and visiting dignitaries are a short ride away. Vital research facilities, Federal agencies, and the IT and government contractors that support them, are located in Baltimore or nearby. Many are enormous consumers and producers of data, and all are eager to leverage the power of ultra high-speed fiber.

The Baltimore-Washington Combined Statistical Area (CSA) is the 4th most populous in the U.S., and it's healthy:

  • Home to approximately 90 percent of Maryland's 5.6 million residents
  • #1 most highly educated workforce
  • 3rd highest income per capita

Maryland is a top-performing state:

  • #1, Best Education System in the Country (Education Week, 2009)
  • #2, State Technology and Science Index (Milken Institute, 2008)
  • #3, New Economy Index (Kauffman, 2008)
  • Longest running AAA Bond rating of any state, dating back to 1961
